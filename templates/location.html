Our practice and match field is located at Spartan Stadium on 1251 S. 10th St. San Jose, CA. 95192

Practice is Mon-Thurs at 6:30PM. The required equipment for practices and games are rugby shorts and jersey, mouthguards, and cleats. Yearly Dues are $350. Players who pay in full within the first 2 weeks of the Fall Semester receive a $50 deduction, for a total of $300 for the year. Dues are essential to the growth of the club and are used to provide players with the following:

  • Two practice jerseys (blue and gold), and one pair of practice shorts/socks
  • Game shorts and socks
  • Hotel expenses for overnight stays at Away games
  • Team meals
  • League and Conference fees
